ABOUT THE WEBINAR

The ability to manage teams, which are able to communicate effectively and overcome barriers to achievement, is a critical skill for any manager.
High performing cohesive teams are created in an environment where managers understand key competences for high performing teams as well as demystify the role systems, culture goals, objectives and leadership play in creating great teams.
This workshop has been designed for managers and people in a supervisory role. You can’t afford to miss this session.


LEARNING OUTCOMES

  • Learn now to create scalable teams (revenue increases without team expenses increasing),
  • Understand key competences for High Performance Teams,
  • Understand the creative fusion of leadership, systems, and culture in delivering great teams,
  • Awareness of key challenges impede teams from optimizing results,
  • Understand how to sustain high performance teams,
